10 minute Yoga Session







Instructions

  1. Start at a seated position with relaxed shoulders
  2. drop left ear towards shoulder, use left arm to pull head towards shoulder and take a 5 deep breaths
  3. release
  4. drop right ear towards right shoulder use right arm to pull head towardsshoudler and take 5 deep breaths
  5. release
  6. Go to table top pose, on hands and knees, with your fingers spread out
  7. Drop belly and curl spine by lifting up head as you inhale
  8. Exhale and curl spine the other way bringing chin to your chest
  9. repeat steps 7 & 8 three times
  10. Come to neutral table top pose. Extend right leg out, while in line with hips.
  11. Swing left arm up and bring down under you. Touching Left shoulder on the ground.
  12. Reach your left hand towards right foot.
  13. Come back up on two hands spread apart and bring right left over and cross over left leg as far left as you can go.
  14. Bring right foot forward, bending knee under shoulder.
  15. Drop hips down.
