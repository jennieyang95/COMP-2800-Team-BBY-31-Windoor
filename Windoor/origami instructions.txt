KIDS - PAPER ANIMALS

PUPPY
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OPOiGpfzj4M&list=WL&index=7&t=10s
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold paper in half on diagonal axis. 
3. Fold the corners together. Unfold.
4. Fold the top and bottom of the head, away from you.
5. Draw eyes, a nose and a mouth!
6. Good job!

FISHY
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=djPgd1m6IMY&list=WL&index=8&t=0s
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold paper in half on diagonal axis.
3. Fold towards the center to get a triangle shape.
4. Hold the right bottom corner of the triangle and fold it toward the center along
the crease. 
5. Good job!

KITTY
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=27UVu2QIhoo
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold paper in half on diagonal axis.
3. Fold the left corner on top of the right and crease the fold. Unfold.
4. Fold each corner upwards to make the ears.
5. Fold the top corner.
6. Draw eyes, a nose and whiskers!
7. Good job!

WHALE
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hjole5KutUA
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold paper in half on diagonal axis. Unfold.
3. Starting from the bottom, fold both sides to meet in the center. Unfold.
4. Starting from the top, fold both sides to meet in the center. Unfold.
5. Make the 2 horizontal creases on the side. 
6. Starting from the right side, fold both top and bottom sides to the center.
7. Repeat Step 6 with the left side.
8. Fold paper in half.
9. Good job!

ADULTS - ORIGAMI

CRANE
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KfnyopxdJXQ&list=WL&index=5
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold it in half by folding the top corner to the bottom corner.
3. Fold part of the top down.
4. Fold flap back to make one of the wings. Fold the other wing back as well.
5. Push the center of the head in and flatten.
6. Voila!

ELEPHANT
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-J39ZorCtJw&list=WL&index=5&t=0s
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold it in half and open it back up. 
3. Fold it right to left and open it back up.
4. Fold the top left corner at an angle to meet the center crease.
5. Fold the paper in half- bring the top edge down to meet the bottom edge.
6. Fold the left side to meet the center crease.
7. Open up the fold. 
8. Make a squash fold.
9. Voila!

ROSE
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JcxixgpRXnQ&list=WL&index=3
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold the paper in half horizontally.
3. Form a Double Blintz.
4. Form a Triple Blintz.
5. Make the petals.
6. Voila!

DRAGON
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8CmCKm20t5A&list=WL&index=2
1. Get a square paper!
2. Fold the paper in half diagonally.
3. Fold this triangle in half again.
4. Open one of the triangle flaps.
5. Fold all sides of square to the crease in the middle.
6. Open the model, and flatten into a crane base.
7. Create a sink fold.
8. Fold the sides down.
9. Fold the head, front feet and back feet.
10. Voila!
