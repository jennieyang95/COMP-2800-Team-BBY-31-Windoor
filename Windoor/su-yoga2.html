20 minute Yoga Session







Instructions

  1. begin standing in a mountain pose. Bring hands together to chest and deepen breath.
  2. Press soles into ground and relax your mind.
  3. inhale and bow head into hands while exhaling
  4. inhale and exhale in this position
  5. as you inhale open arms and reach upwards and come down and touch ground bring handsto upper thigs and stretch
  6. exhale
  7. inhale back into mountain pose with hands together
  8. exhale and reach upwards
  9. go into plank pose and slowly lower your knees until they touch the ground.
  10. exhale and bring chin towards chest
  11. inhale and drop belly and lift head
  12. lift knees and lft hips into downward dog pose
  13. bring right knee to left elbow
  14. bring left knee to right elbow
  15. repeat 5 times
