ADULTS - YOGA&MEDITATION

BREATHE
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5DqTuWve9t8
1. Sit or lie flat in a comfortable position.
2. Put one hand on your belly just below your ribs and the other hand on your chest.
3. Take a deep breath in through your nose, and let your belly push your hand out. Your chest should not move.
4. Breathe out through pursed lips as if you were whistling. Feel the hand on your belly go in, and use it to push all the air out.
5. Do this breathing 3 to 10 times. Take your time with each breath.
6. Notice how you feel at the end of the exercise.

DOWNWARD DOG
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YqOqM79McYY
1. Begin in Table position.
2. Raise the body up and back into posture.
3. Hold and breathe for 10 minutes.

CHILD'S POSE
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l-mtV34Afok
1. Kneel on the floor with your toes together and your knees hip-width apart. Rest your palms on top of your thighs.
2. On an exhale, lower your torso between your knees. Extend your arms alongside your torso with your palms facing down. 
   Relax your shoulders toward the ground.
3. Hold and breathe for 10 minutes.
